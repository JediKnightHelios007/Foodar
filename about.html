How It Works!

REGISTER FOR AN ACCOUNT

Register for an account. The first thing you need to do is register for an account. After registering you will be able to submit requests for pickup and have your food delivered to your door!

ORDER YOUR FOOD

Place an order for food item from a local restaurant and the system will automatically find the nearest driver at the best cost.

CONFIRM YOUR REQUEST

Beofre any request is submitted, you have the chance to review it and make any changes or cancellations.
